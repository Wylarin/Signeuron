Creative AdBlock

This project was created to fight capitalistic system and let Internet users to be free from manipulating their minds. Creative AdBlock helps to avoid annoying advertisements by blocking links on big-corps-pages. It also allows to replace advertisements with beautiful pictures.

Prohojdenye polyteha

Prohojdenye polyteha is a game written on C++. It was made just for fun :) There are two plots in this game, one for every main character: Jenya and Vasya. If you are playing for Vasya, you should constantly run over the Polyteh and collect different stuff to get main artefact - automat. If you have collected > 50% on every level (semester) you get the coolest artefact - Red Diploma. Well, if you are playing for Jenya you must be very clever because her game plot is full of puzzles. You also can create your character and his/her story and share it with your friends who are also playing in Prohojdenye polyteha.
